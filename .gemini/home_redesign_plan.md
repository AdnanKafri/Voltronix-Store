### DESIGN SYSTEM IMPROVEMENTS:

#### Typography Scale:
- Hero Title: 3.5rem → 4rem
- Section Title: 2.5rem → 2.25rem
- Card Title: 1.3rem → 1.15rem
- Body: 1rem → 0.95rem

#### Spacing System:
- Section Padding: 6rem → 5rem (desktop), 4rem → 3.5rem (mobile)
- Card Gap: 2rem → 1.75rem
- Element Margin: Consistent 1.5rem scale

#### Card Redesign:
- Reduce card height by 15%
- Lighter shadows
- Thinner borders
- More white space

#### Electric/Tech Accents:
- Subtle grid patterns in backgrounds
- Neon accent lines on section dividers
- Glow effects on hover
- Electric blue gradients
- Animated circuit-like patterns

### COLOR REFINEMENTS:
- Background: Pure white → Subtle off-white (#fafbfc)
- Section Alt BG: Light gray (#f8f9fa)
- Accent: Electric blue glow effects
- Text: Darker, higher contrast
